Membership Levels

Non Profit Membership Level

Non Profit Membership Level

Price: $0/mo

Designed for nonprofit organizations to stay connected with the community.

Benefits:

  • Inclusion in the member directory.
  • Access to general community events.
  • Networking opportunities with other nonprofits and local businesses.
  • Monthly newsletter access.
  • Listed in a special section for nonprofits on the websiteListed in a special section for nonprofits on the website.

Bronze Membership Level

Bronze Membership Level

Price: $5/mo

Ideal for small businesses looking to increase visibility and make connections.

Benefits:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Enhanced profile in the member directory.
  • 1 promotional post per quarter on Chamber social media.
  • Access to basic training and webinars.
  • 10% discount on paid events.
  • “Chamber Member” badge for website or storefront display.

Silver Membership Level

Silver Membership Level

Price: $10/mo

Perfect for growing businesses seeking greater exposure and leadership opportunities.

Benefits:

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • 1 featured spotlight position per year on the homepage.
  • Logo featured in monthly newsletters.
  • 2 promotional posts per quarter on Chamber social media.
  • 25% discount on paid events.
  • Priority access to Silver+ exclusive events.
  • Opportunity to lead workshops or speaking sessions.

Gold Membership Level

Gold Membership Level

Price: $15/mo

Designed for established businesses that want to lead, influence, and maximize their presence.

Benefits:

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Monthly mentions in newsletters and social media.
  • Permanent logo placement in the website footer.
  • Exclusive landing page on the Chamber’s website.
  • Priority seating and early access to major events.
  • 50% discount on all paid events.
  • Premium advertising in newsletters and social media.
  • Opportunity to be a primary sponsor for Chamber events.
